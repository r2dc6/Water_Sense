About WaterSense

WaterSense System is a device and webpage application for automating the process of water analysis of deep well groundwater by implementing computer vision and robotics. The systemâ€™s job is that from the water sample, it shall analyze the water quality factors such as pH level, Manganese, hydrogen sulfide, nitrate, nitrite, alkalinity, and hardness level content of the sample that will based its results from the color coded chart. The process will only take approximately 5 minutes. Moreover, the system will also test the possibilities of bacterial contamination in the said water sample. The process of checking for coliform bacteria will take more than or equal to 48 hours or less than 72 hours.
